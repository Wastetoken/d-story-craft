Welcome to Scroll Studio

This is a camera rail system that lets you create cinematic camera movements through 3D space.

Scroll down to see how it works and learn how to build your own camera paths.

👇 Scroll to begin the tutorial

Step 1: Find Your First Shot

The camera is currently moving along a pre-built rail, focusing on the green cube.

In Scroll Studio, you'd start by moving your camera around the scene to find the perfect initial angle.

Press A to enter Free Roam Mode and explore

Use your mouse to look around and find interesting angles of the 3D objects.

Step 2: Capture Your First Node

Once you've found your first shot, you'd hit Capture.

This places the first node - a point that marks where your camera should be and what it's looking at.

Notice the small pink/purple spheres along the cyan rail? Those are the captured nodes.

The camera rail remembers both position and target

Step 3: Build Your Rail

Watch as the camera smoothly transitions to focus on the orange cone.

When you capture a second node, Scroll Studio creates a rail between them.

The camera smoothly interpolates between positions, creating cinematic movement.

More nodes = Longer, more complex camera movements

Step 4: Add More Nodes

Now the camera is targeting the red sphere.

Each time you hit Capture, you extend your camera rail. The more nodes you add, the more intricate your camera path becomes.

Each node stores:

• Camera position in 3D space

• What the camera is looking at

• Smooth transitions to the next node

Step 5: Smart Focus Targeting

The camera is now looking at the blue torus.

The key feature: the rail focuses on whatever you're aiming at when you hit Capture.

This means each node can target a different object, creating dynamic storytelling through your camera movement.

Press I in Free Roam to edit nodes

Step 6: Edit Your Rail

Made a mistake? Want to adjust your path?

In Free Roam mode (press A), you can enable Edit Mode (press I).

The nodes turn yellow, and you can click and drag them to reshape your camera path in real-time.

Try it: Press A, then I, then drag the yellow nodes!

You're Ready!

That's the Scroll Studio workflow:

1. Move your camera to find a shot

2. Capture to place a node

3. Repeat to build your rail

4. Edit nodes to refine your path

5. Scroll through your cinematic experience
