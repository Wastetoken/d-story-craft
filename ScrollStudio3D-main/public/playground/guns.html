  • Material The HK MP5 is primarily built from stamped high-strength steel for its receiver and components, combined with durable polymers for grips, forends, and stocks, featuring a cold hammer-forged steel barrel for longevity and accuracy, with some modern variations or aftermarket parts using materials like 6061 aluminum for lighter weight.

It is known for its accuracy, reliability, and controllable recoil due to its closed-bolt, roller‑delayed blowback operating system.
